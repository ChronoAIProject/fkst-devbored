Council

who deliberates, how, and how well

Council

linear-council.html

Three tabs. Personas — define the lenses (teleology, parsimony, fidelity, natural-ownership, proportional-containment, security, meta-judge), each with a brief, model, effort, and where it sits; retired ones stay in a library. Flows — what happens to a piece of work: seats per step, what counts as agreement, veto seats, round budgets, and where work goes when they disagree. Scores — is a persona earning its seat: objected / upheld / changed-mind / caught-alone, including retired ones for comparison.

linear-council-debate.html

The evidence behind the scores. Pick a voice and read only what it said across rounds — the speaker filter is live. Shows the meta-judge narrowing the question, seats that changed position after narrowing, the security veto in review, and your human comment in the thread.
